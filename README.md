## Entry Points for Contributors

**Start anywhere. Fork freely. No permission needed.**

### Story Entry Points

- Write a scene where a young activist discovers a secret letter from President Asante's revolutionary past, addressed to them personally.
- Draft a monologue from Ama Serwah's perspective, reflecting on the moment she realized her brother was no longer just 'Kofi'.
- Create a dialogue between Minister Jojo Mensah and the Chinese engineer, negotiating over the unstable mine shafts.
- Write the 'confessional' anniversary speech President Asante *wishes* he could give, using Ama's margin notes as a guide.
